About Me

Little overview and motivation for my persuit of the Data Science profession


A couple of years back


I was studying Information Technology, software development at the Central University of Technology over five years ago. Even though things did not go as planned with me receiving my diploma, the experience opened me up to the potential the technological sector has, which made me even more enthusiastic about finding my place in it.


Now


Fast-forward 2019, I stumbled upon Data Science. The ability to work and produce meaningful insights using technology, enhanced by statistical methods were so enticing I could not resist. In 2021 I applied for the Data Science program with Umuzi, and I am making a dream a reality in pursuit of the Data Scientist accolade.


And in the Future


I see Data Science as a tool with means to achieve. The biggest goal I have is to find a way to help people through the skills I attain in my journey towards being a data scientist. I believe, especially in this beautiful country of ours, South Africa, that there is so much potential for growth and in many different spheres. What would be more pleasing than to be able to gather skills and use them effectively to help improve it's people and their standard of living?
