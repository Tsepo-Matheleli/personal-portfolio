About Me


Little overview and motivation for my persuit of the Data Science profession

A couple of years back

I was studying Information Technology, software development at the Central University of Technology,that is over 5 years ago. Even though things did not go as planned with jme receiving my diploma, the experience opened me up to the potential the technological sector has, which made me even more enthusiastic about finding my place in it.


Now

Fast-forward 2019, I stumbled upon Data Science. The ability to work and produce meaningful insights using technology inhanced by statistical methods was so enticing I could not resist. In 2021 I ended up applying for the Data Science program with Umuzi and I am making a dream a reality, in pursuit of the Data Scientist accolade.


And in the Future

My honest truth is I see Data Science as a tool with means to achieve. The biggest goal I have is to find a way to help people through the skills I attain in my journey towards being a data scientist. I believe, especially in this beautiful country of ours that there is so much potential for growth and on many different spheres. What would be more pleasing than to be able to gather skills and use them effectively to help improve people and the way in which we live?
